Our Story

From Local Routes to Global Networks

Aurum Freight International was founded with one mission — to make global logistics seamless, transparent and reliable for businesses of every size. What started as a regional freight operation has grown into a full-scale international logistics company serving clients across 150+ countries.

The name Aurum — Latin for gold — reflects our commitment to gold-standard service in everything we do. From the moment your cargo leaves its origin to the second it arrives at its destination, we are accountable.
